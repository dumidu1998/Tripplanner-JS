About Us





This Web app is about helping university students specifically Monash Students to help plan their travel when transitioning to universities from one country to another.
Our app makes it easier for students as the student can select which airport to depart from and pick the country the student wishes to go to.
Our app will then display a number of routes in which the student can select the route they prefer.
It also filters all domestic flights so that each route is unique.
